What We Are

We are a club that seeks to build a family of youth leaders that centers its activities around the needs of the members. Fun is always ingrained in our events as we mentor the primers through experimental learning. Through it, you will discover more about yourselves.

“Where many CCA clubs build communities, we build families.”

In our club, we have a wide range of platforms through which our primers can experience the lifestyle of the old back in the kampong days, or go wild as a NDP motivator. Your hearts will be touched by our community outreach through BBSG (Boys Brigade Share-a-gift) and Service Learning trips to overseas.
